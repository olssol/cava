VISIT: Phase I Dose Escalation Design

A Bayesian Phase I trial design that allows simultaneous evaluation of safety and immunogenicity outcomes in the context of vaccine studies.

BASIM: Phase II Two-Stage Design

A batch-effect adjusted Simon's two-stage design for Phase II cancer vaccine studies.

DELPHI: Deescaltion Phase I Design

A Phase I dose deescaltion trial design that allows simultaneous evaluation of safety and immunogenicity outcomes for cancer vaccines.
